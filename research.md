## Confirmed for publication

The project brief and visible public Facebook page identify 319Junk as serving residential, commercial, and industrial removal needs. They show the office phone as 319-461-6329, Eastern and Southeast Iowa coverage, trailer rentals, estate cleanouts, free estimates, loading handled by the company, conditional same/next-day availability, and a $140 minimum junk-removal charge.

The public page was directly opened in the in-app Browser. A Facebook login overlay prevented reliable inspection of About details, recent post text, individual photo ownership, matching before/after projects, and any email address.

## Photo inventory

No photos were copied. The publicly visible cover appears to include a truck/trailer and service categories, but ownership, original file access, and suitability could not be verified cleanly. Version one therefore uses brand-led placeholders. Potential future assets: trailer/vehicle, team at a job, estate cleanout, project debris, and verified matched before/after pairs.

## Not confirmed; do not publish

Owner or employee names, founding story, years in business, street address, hours, email address, team size, insurance/licensing claims, guarantees, disposal or donation practices, service radius, exact trailer size/capacity/duration/pricing, accepted/prohibited items, reviews, ratings, coordinates, and unconditional same-day service.

## Design findings

Current industry guidance consistently prioritizes immediate service clarity, local coverage, price/process transparency, real work proof, obvious mobile contact controls, and fast loading. The implementation uses those principles without copying a competitor or inventing proof.
